What is Vijual?

Vijual (pronounced “visual”) is the next evolution of Odin Chart

Odin Chart is an Adobe XD plugin that makes data-driven chart design and placement easy for designers.

Vijual expands Odin Chart’s core functionality to new platforms and has been redesigned to make every step of the chart design process more flexible:


  • Easier data import that accepts more data sources
  • Custom color palettes
  • Reusable chart designs
  • Greater control over chart customizations
  • Easily share generated charts
