Data-Driven Design

Odin Chart will support Adobe XD and Sketch. Import data from spreadsheet files and urls to support your workflow.

About

Odin Chart is a plugin currently under development for Adobe XD. Future iterations will include support for web-based design and support for Sketch. Development is generously supported by the Adobe Fund for Design. Odin Chart was created and developed by Tyler Wolf.
